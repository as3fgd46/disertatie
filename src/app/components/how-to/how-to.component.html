How to use the web app

  • Click on the Walls button and drag on the grid to create walls
  • To remove walls, click the Remove Walls button and drag on existing walls to remove them
  • To change the start position of the search, choose the Change Start button and click anywhere on the grid
  • To change the end position of the search, choose the Change End button and click anywhere on the grid
  • The pink buttons represent algorithms you can use to search. Click on any one of them
  • To create a random arrangement of walls, click on the Random Walls button
  • To try out a maze, chooze a sample maze from the dropdown
  • Click Reset to revert all changes on the grid
